Nachos

These easy nachos are loaded with zesty beef, toppings and of course lots of shredded cheese. Simply fill a sheet pan and bake until golden and bubbly!

Below are instructions on how to make them.

    Ingredients
  • Tortilla Chips
  • Cheese
  • Meat
    Directions
  1. Prepare meat mixture (if using): Brown ground beef, stir in taco seasoning, water, and black beans. Simmer until the water has evaporated.
  2. Layer on a pan: Layer chips with cheese, beef and bean mixture, and green onions per recipe below.
  3. Bake: Bake until the cheese is melted and bubbly. Remove from oven and load em up!

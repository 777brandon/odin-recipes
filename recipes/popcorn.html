Popcorn

This is popcorn, corn kernels heated up and popped with butter, salt, and other condiments of your choosing.

Below are instructions on how to make it.

    Ingredients
  • Popcorn Kernels
  • Salt
  • Butter
    Directions
  1. First you heat up a pot at medium-high heat with some butter at the bottom.
  2. Then you add your popcorn kernels.
  3. Now begin to stir your popcorn kernels into the butter.
  4. Once the kernels start to sizzle and sound ready to pop, put your lid on your pot.
  5. Watch and listen to your popcorn
